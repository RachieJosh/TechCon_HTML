History

The Evolution of TechCon: From Humble Beginnings to a Global Phenomenon

  • TechCon was founded in 1995 by a group of tech pioneers who wanted to create a platform for sharing cutting-edge ideas and technologies.

  • The first event was held in a modest venue in San Francisco, with just 200 attendees.

Growth and Expansion

  • By the early 2000s, TechCon had become a key event in the tech calendar, attracting over 5,000 attendees.

  • Major companies started sponsoring the event, and the venue moved to larger convention centers.

Present Day

  • Today, TechCon is attended by over 50,000 people annually, offering a diverse range of talks, workshops, and networking opportunities.

  • The conference continues to innovate, incorporating virtual elements and interactive experiences.

Mission

Vision

  • To be the leading platform for technological innovation and collaboration, fostering the exchange of ideas and driving forward the tech industry.

Goals

  • Inspire Innovation: Showcase the latest advancements and breakthroughs in technology.

  • Promote Collaboration: Create opportunities for networking and partnerships among attendees.

  • Educate and Inform: Provide a platform for learning through talks, workshops, and hands-on sessions.

  • Support Diversity: Encourage participation from diverse groups and support underrepresented communities in tech.
